# MicroTutor FAQ

## What is MicroTutor?
MicroTutor is an on-demand micro-tutoring platform that connects students with tutors for instant help in specific subjects. Students can post queries, tutors can accept them, and they can collaborate using an interactive whiteboard and chat system.

## How does it work?
1. **For Students**: Register, post a query with subject and subtopic, wait for tutors to accept, choose a tutor, and start a session with whiteboard and chat.
2. **For Tutors**: Complete your profile, set your rate, view queries matching your specialties, accept queries, and help students using the whiteboard and chat.

## What subjects are available?
The platform supports various subjects including Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and more. Tutors can specify their specialties when setting up their profile.

## How are sessions priced?
Tutors set their own rate per 10-minute session. Students can view tutor profiles and rates before accepting a tutor for their query.

## Is there a free trial?
Currently, the platform operates on a pay-per-session model. Each session is billed based on the tutor's rate.

## How do I register as a student?
Navigate to the student registration page, fill in your details, and create an account. You'll then be able to post queries and connect with tutors.

## How do I become a tutor?
Navigate to the tutor setup page, complete your profile with your bio, education, specialties, and set your rate. Once your profile is complete, you can start accepting student queries.

## What features are available during a session?
During a session, both students and tutors have access to:
- Real-time chat messaging
- Interactive whiteboard for drawing and collaboration
- Session management tools

## Can I cancel a session?
Session cancellation policies may vary. Please contact support for specific cancellation terms.

## Is my data secure?
Yes, we use JWT-based authentication and secure password hashing to protect your account and personal information.

## How do I contact support?
For questions or support, please contact the development team through the platform's contact options.
